ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

Three days International Conference addresses the rapid strides and technological advancements currently witnessed in the fields of ICRTEST. The conference aspires to exhibit the technical excellence of budding technocrats, research scholars, and representatives from academia and industry. This conference aims to bring together the best of globally renewed research professionals. English is the official language of the conference. We welcome paper submissions. Papers presented at the conference will be submitted for inclusion in Conference proceedings. This conference concentrates on various fields of engineering like

  1. Aeronautical & Aerospace Engineering
  2. Biomedical Engineering
  3. Computer science Engineering
  4. Civil Engineering
  5. Mechanical Engineering
  6. Electronics & Communication Engineering
  7. Applied Science & Mathematics
